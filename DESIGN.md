# Design System: VMC AI Complaint Dashboard

## Overview
Design tokens for the Vadodara Municipal Corporation AI Complaint Dashboard.

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)

## Color Palette
- **Mode:** Light
- **Primary:** `#1173d4` — actions, active nav, CTA buttons
- **Primary Light:** `#e8f2fc` — stat card backgrounds
- **Primary Dark:** `#0b5aa8` — hover states
- **Surface:** `#f8f9fb` — page background
- **Card Surface:** `#ffffff` — cards, table, panels

### Status Colors
| Status      | Background | Text      |
|-------------|------------|-----------|
| Pending     | `#fef3cd`  | `#856404` |
| In Progress | `#d1ecf1`  | `#0c5460` |
| Resolved    | `#d4edda`  | `#155724` |

### Category Badge Colors
| Category     | Background     | Text          |
|--------------|----------------|---------------|
| Street Light | `yellow-50`    | `yellow-700`  |
| Water Supply | `sky-50`       | `sky-700`     |
| Garbage      | `emerald-50`   | `emerald-700` |
| Drainage     | `violet-50`    | `violet-700`  |
| Road         | `orange-50`    | `orange-700`  |

## Shape & Geometry
- **Border Radius:** 8px (`rounded-lg`)
- Cards, buttons, inputs, badges all use 8px rounding.

## Spacing
- Page max-width: `max-w-7xl` (80rem)
- Section gap: `1.5rem` (24px)
- Card padding: `1rem` (16px)
